Ph.D. Student @ Seoul National University

Jeongwoo Shin

Hi! I'm a Ph.D. student at the Graduate School of Data Science, Seoul National University, where I work in the VIP Lab advised by Prof. Joonseok Lee. I also completed my M.S. in Data Science in the same lab.

My research focuses on generative modeling, particularly diffusion-, flow-, and control-based image and video generation. Recently, I have been working on long video generation and one-step distillation.

I am actively seeking internship opportunities.
